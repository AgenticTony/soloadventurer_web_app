# Sprint 4: Messaging & Feed

**SoloAdventurer Web App - Sprint 4**
**Duration**: 2 weeks
**Status**: 📋 Planned
**Dependencies**: Sprint 7.5 (UI/UX Overhaul — for feed layout and post card anatomy)

---

## Sprint Goal

Build the social engagement features including activity feeds, content sharing, and notification systems. The feed layout and post card anatomy will be established in Sprint 7.5; this sprint builds the functional data layer and interactions on top of that structure.

---

## Key Features

### Activity Feed
- Global and friend-based activity feeds
- Trip check-ins and status updates
- Photo and media sharing
- Feed algorithms and filtering
- **From UI/UX review**: Content-type filters (Latest · Popular · Photos · Trip Updates · Tips)
- **From UI/UX review**: "For You · Following · Nearby · Trip Buddies" sub-tabs

### Social Interactions
- Like, comment, and share functionality
- Mention and tagging system
- Post privacy controls
- Content moderation tools
- **From UI/UX review**: "Say hi" / "Connect" button on every post card (connection trigger)

### Notifications
- Real-time notification system
- Push notifications (mobile + web)
- Email notification preferences
- Notification center and history

### Post Card Anatomy (From UI/UX Review)
- Avatar + name + location tag + "currently in [city]" status + timestamp
- Body text + photo carousel
- **"Say hi" button** directly on every post (connection CTA, not just engagement)
- Reactions + comments
- Warm accent border for posts from users in your current city
